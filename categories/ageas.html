Company Overview

Hello there.

We're EastWest Ageas Insurance. We used to be called Troo, but we've refreshed recently to carry both our parent firms' names—East West and Ageas.

We were founded in 2015 and are the first insurance company in the world to have an end-to-end integrated IT platform in the cloud. Our technology enables us to offer our customers a seamless experience and customized financial solutions.

It's our passion and commitment to help Filipinos plan for a better tomorrow so they can live their best lives today.

EastWest Ageas Insurance is a lifetime partnership between EastWest Bank, one of the country's leading companies with over 400+ branches, and Ageas, an award-winning international insurance group with a heritage spanning 200 years.

Our solutions are designed with your needs in mind. So, they're easy to understand, simple to manage, and work with your budget.
