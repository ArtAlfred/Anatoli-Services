Sysgen

  • Software Engineer
  • Silkroad Technology Philippines, Inc.
  • Taguig City
  • Posted 2 hours ago
  • Job Highlights
  • Flexible work environment
  • Career Growth Opportunities
  • Great company culture and people-centric
  • PRIMARY PURPOSE
  • The Software Engineer is responsible for the overall technology and architecture of SilkRoad products and services. As part of the Development team, the Software Engineer IV will be involved in the design, coding, unit testing, code review, and own the architecture of SilkRoad services and applications.
  • REQUIREMENTS:
  • All employees of SilkRoad are expected to use good business judgment and appropriate discretion and decision making while performing responsibilities of the position.
  • Owns cross-team shared technology and architecture.
  • Works with head of development to choose new technologies and promote culture/process.
  • Does serious research to evaluate and test options.
  • Understands implications (and trade-offs) of reliability, scalability, operational costs, ease of adoption by organization, recruiting, etc.
  • Evaluate system-wide platform decisions and set long-term company-wide technical strategy.
  • Potentially work both as individual contributors on a feature team and as architectural reviewers working with the head of development.
  • The responsibilities listed above are representative of the main duties; other duties may be assigned or altered based on business need.
  • COMPETENCIES & SKILLS
  • Core Competencies & Skills:
  • Results driven, highly engaged professional; uses discretionary effort to meet/exceed desired results in a fast paced environment.
  • Passionate team member; collaborative; cultivates an energizing environment and is committed to team success.
  • Supports culture of customer appreciation; plays an active role in creating a positive customer experience.
  • Demonstrates effective communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Possesses appropriate knowledge and skills; understands company, process, products, job duties, and necessary work methods.
  • Contributes to a climate of innovation and continuous improvement to achieve excellence.
  • Demonstrates personal excellence, integrity and accountability.
  • Identifies ways to continuously improve own and/or company performance.
  • Job Specific Competencies & Skills:
  • Proficient with cloud software development and agile development practices.
  • Knowledge of cloud platforms.
  • Experience with a variety of technology areas.
  • Ability to work independently between periodic check-ins.
  • Estimates timelines effectively; identifies and communicates roadblocks in timely manner; takes operational excellence seriously.
  • Understands implications (and trade-offs) of reliability, scalability, operational costs, ease of adoption by organization, recruiting, etc.
  • Can evaluate system-wide platform decisions and set long-term company-wide technical strategy.
  • Has deep technical expertise in business-critical areas.
  • Experience working well with senior leadership.
  • Ability to identify and positively encourage areas of growth and improvement within assigned team.
  • Ability to engage team members at all levels and maintain open communication stream.
  • EDUCATION & EXPERIENCE
  • Minimum 10 years’ experience developing commercial software in a cloud environment.
  • Minimum of 8 years’ experience developing independently in a variety of technology areas and owning functional areas.
  • Experience with Microsoft stack (C#, SQL Server, ASP.NET)
  • Experience with React, Node, Angular
  • Experience with agile development practices like Scrum and Kanban
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or other technical discipline, or equivalent experience.
